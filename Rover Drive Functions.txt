Rover Drive 4 bits
0-15
0 - kill, dead stop
1 - Normal Forward
2 - Normal Forward Left 
3 - Normal Forward Right
4 - Normal Reverse
5 - Normal Reverse Left
6 - Normal Reverse Right
7 - Fast Forward
8 - Fast Forward Left
9 - Fast Forward Right
10 - Fast Reverse
11 - Fast Reverse Left
12 - Fast Reverse Right
13 - Stop Left
14 - Stop Right
15 - Slow-stop (Ramp-down of speed instead of instant stop)
